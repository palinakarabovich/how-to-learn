The main problems

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to study the material correctly. We are preparing for exams. We train ourselves to solve the same problems again and again in order to pass the test better, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge and we don't remember anything after. All information quickly disappears and does not bring any benefit

Conclusion: you need to be able to study effectively, but for some reason nobody teaches us how to do it. What can we do then?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting.

How to study?

Five techniques from Barbara Oakley

  • Two kinds of attention

    Deep knowledge arises by changing between focused and diffuse thinking during the process.

  • Recall

    Recall what you have learned - this will allow you to connect diffrent parts of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    As you listen to your teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

  • Illusion of competence

    Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it just seems to you that you understand a new topic.

The Barbara Oakley Story.

Since childhood, Barbara didn't like mathematics. She considered herself a complete humanist, and far from being the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise, so successfully that she was promoted to commander. But for promotion, you had to take math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach. It turned out that if something is "hard to get" to you, your knowledge gained by "suffering" is much deeper than that of those to whom everything is clear at first sight.
